- Spawn creatures with mutations

- Dwarves tech nearly as fast (1.3x!)

- Each tech level increases happiness

- Create 2 neighboring kingdoms

- Diplomacy degrades naturally over time

### Tip 6: Zoom & Observe
- Zoom in to see individual creatures
- See their mutations and behaviors
- Creatures with wings actually fly!
- Zoom out to see whole civilization

### Tip 7: Environmental Storytelling
- Terraform specific areas as kingdoms grow
- Create bridges between islands for human kingdoms
- Create mountains to separate orc and elf kingdoms
- Use lava as natural barriers
