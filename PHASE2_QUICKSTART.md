### 3. Civilizations Building
Watch civilizations develop automatically!

**How It Happens:**
- Humanoid creatures (human, elf, dwarf, orc, undead) will build houses, roads, and town halls
- No player action needed - just watch!
- Buildings appear over time as your civilization grows

**What Each Building Does:**
- **Houses** (brown peaked roof): Population centers, show population number above
- **Roads** (gray path): Connect buildings together, visual pathways
- **Town Halls** (large stone building with flag): Government centers

**To Speed Up Building:**
- Create lots of humanoid creatures (click human/elf/dwarf buttons)
- More creatures = more building activity
- Buildings cluster near each other naturally

**Try This Scenario:**
1. Spawn 20 humans in one area
2. Watch them build houses and roads around each other
3. Change to another race and spawn them elsewhere
4. See different civilizations develop independently

---

### 4. Natural Creature Spawning

**What to Notice:**
- New creatures appear on their own
- Population stays balanced (doesn't grow infinitely)
- Different species spawn in appropriate terrain
- Fish spawn in water, birds can spawn anywhere

**Population Limits:**
- Maximum 150 creatures total
- Each species has limits (humans max 30, wolves max 15, etc.)
- System respects these automatically

**To See It Best:**
1. Start game with medium or large world
2. Don't spawn anything initially
3. Wait 30 seconds - creatures will appear!
4. Watch populations grow and stabilize

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

**Creatures not building houses?**
- Make sure you have humanoid creatures (not animals)
- Building is random - may take 2-5 minutes to see first house
- Spawn 20+ humanoids to speed up building

**Spawned creatures disappear?**
- They die from starvation (no food system yet) after ~1000 frames
- Create new creatures to maintain population
- Watch stats panel to see decline

**Road tiles not appearing?**
- Roads blend with grass background
- Look for the gray paved path with gold lines
- They appear between houses

**Buildings covering creatures?**
- This is intentional - buildings render on top
- Creatures still move around buildings normally
